# Cookies and Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: 2024-10-07

**Peragon Games INC.** ("We", "Us", "Our", or the "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Cookies and Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you use our website, software, and services (collectively, the "Services"). By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy.

## 1. **Information We Collect**
We collect different types of personal data to provide and improve our Services:
- **Personal Information**: Includes name, email address, billing information, and other details you provide directly when creating an account or purchasing our Services.
- **Automatically Collected Information**: This includes IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior collected through cookies and similar technologies.
- **Usage Data**: Data about how you use and interact with our Services, including log files, access times, and page views.

## 2. **How We Use Your Information**
We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and improve our Services.
- To process transactions and manage accounts.
- To communicate with you, including sending important notices, updates, and marketing materials (where permitted).
- To analyze usage and trends to enhance user experience.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## 3. **Cookies and Tracking Technologies**
We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information about your interaction with our Services. Cookies help us improve the user experience by storing preferences, enabling features, and analyzing traffic.

- **Types of Cookies We Use**:
  - **Essential Cookies**: Necessary for the proper functioning of the website.
  - **Performance Cookies**: Help us understand how you interact with our Services.
  - **Functional Cookies**: Enable personalization and enhanced functionality.
  - **Targeting/Advertising Cookies**: Used to deliver personalized advertisements and to track performance.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our website.

- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Request that we limit how your data is processed.
